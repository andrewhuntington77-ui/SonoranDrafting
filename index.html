About Sonoran Drafting & Design

Sonoran Drafting and Design LLC specializes in drafting and design services for residential construction throughout the Southwest. We focus on creating energy efficient, passive solar custom homes that work in harmony with our desert climate.

Our comprehensive services include plan submittal for permitting, construction documents for remodels and new builds, passive solar consultation, and production of detailed 3D renderings. We understand the unique challenges of desert construction and design homes that maximize comfort while minimizing energy consumption.
